Local councils with an active Business Improvement District (BID) will benefit most from Pikhaya Smart Streets.

Local councils and BIDs receive structured analysis to improve the way they manage urban renewal projects and attract investment to their city. They can compare their retail and commercial tenant mix to other cities, and evaluate the impact of changes on occupancy over time. Adding in their footfall data can reveal how business activity varies with pedestrian density and potentially offer insight into new ways to attract people.

This page presents a proposed service offering as well as a mockup of that view. This is not a product offer.
