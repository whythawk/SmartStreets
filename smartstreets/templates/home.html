Pikhaya Smart Streets offers market intelligence to assess the business potential in empty commercial property in urban centres. We aggregate open data on local consumer purchasing behaviour and pedestrian footfall, as well as existing local business rent and salary expenditure, to offer insight into revenue potential for different categories of new business starts, and in cities across the UK.

Pikhaya is created and developed by Whythawk, an open data consulting, software and infrastructure development company. We deliver capacity-building for open knowledge data services for governments, institutions and organisations.

This site presents a very early proof-of-concept (pre-alpha) based on our pilot in Leeds and Sunderland.
