Pikhaya Smart Streets offers market intelligence to assess the business potential in empty commercial property in urban centres. We aggregate local consumer purchasing behaviour and pedestrian footfall, as well as existing local business rent and salary expenditure, to offer insight into revenue potential for different categories of new business starts, and in cities across the UK.

This site presents a very early prototype (pre-alpha) based on our pilot in Leeds and Sunderland.
