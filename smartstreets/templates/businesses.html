Our planned service offering

Business seekers receive free analysis on specific business sites, links to available long- and short-term rentals, and can search and filter both existing and vacant businesses by revenue, rental cost and rental area. They can compare and assess business revenue potential between different cities, and create a custom profile to receive alerts related to matching business opportunities, including sites offered for long- and short-term leases.

This page presents a proposed service offering as well as a mockup of that view. This is not a product offer.
