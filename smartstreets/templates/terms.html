This site (“Pikhaya Smart Streets”) provides business intelligence analysis to encourage assessment of the revenue potential in empty commercial properties in urban centres. There are potential sources of error in our data and analysis and you should complete thorough due diligence yourself prior to starting any business or signing any lease agreement.

Data and analysis have been prepared from publicly available open data, standard methodology for business risk analysis, and other sources believed to be reliable. The data and analysis presented on this site is not intended to be comprehensive and is for information purposes only.

All data and analysis are licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International.

Data are sourced from the following providers:

  • Household expenditure, ONS
  • Regional Household Income, ONS
  • Commercial rent valuation and floor area, Valuations Office Agency (VOA)
  • Employment Densities Guide, Homes and Communities Agency
  • Specific city council rates and vacancy data, from the council concerned
  • Specific city footfall telemetry, from the council concerned
